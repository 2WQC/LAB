# 'DATA' LAB

In the DATA Lab, new analyses, concepts, approaches, and systems are tested.

## Overview

The lab follows a highly technical approach to data work:

- **Understanding the data**  
  The dataset is initially explored and analyzed to gain a clear understanding of its structure, patterns, and characteristics.

- **Data preparation**  
  Data is cleaned, transformed, and prepared to support specific concepts and methodological approaches.

- **Application**  
  New concepts and methods are applied to the prepared data to evaluate their effectiveness and derive insights.

## Working philosophy

In general, I frequently work with the same datasets over time.
This allows for a deep understanding of the data and helps place new results into a consistent and meaningful context.
